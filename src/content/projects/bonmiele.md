## Overview

Bonmiele’s visual system was conceived to communicate rarity and care, a product shaped by patience, expertise and devotion.<br class="hide-mobile" />
The brand identity combines a refined, contemporary tone with classic cues, creating an elegant presence across packaging and communication.
