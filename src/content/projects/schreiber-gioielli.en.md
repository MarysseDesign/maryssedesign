## Brand Identity & Digital Positioning

<p>
  Our goal was to define a renewed visual “wardrobe” for online communication able to<br class="hide-mobile" />
  translate the brand’s heritage into a contemporary language. <br class="hide-mobile" />
  The direction builds on key pillars such as craftsmanship, tradition and refined taste,<br class="hide-mobile" />
  balancing simplicity with precious materiality and an editorial approach to imagery.
</p>

## Monogram as Narrator

<p>
  Starting from a historic symbol, we designed a new monogram that became a central <br class="hide-mobile" />
  storytelling element for the brand across social media and DEM. <br class="hide-mobile" />
  It reinterprets the brand’s iconic daisy into a minimal, contemporary mark conceived <br class="hide-mobile" />
  to function as a visual narrator and remain recognizable even without the wordmark.
</p>

## Art Direction & Social Content System

<p>
  A full art direction process was defined to establish a consistent visual language on social media: <br class="hide-mobile" />
  pre-shoot planning, on-set creative supervision, content selection and post-production,<br class="hide-mobile" />
  followed by the development of editorial plans and publishing calendars.
</p>

<p>
  A key narrative concept in the imagery is light: Schreiber’s jewelry is treated as “creations of light”, with lighting <br class="hide-mobile" />
  becoming an active element to express brand values and enhance materials, details and craftsmanship.<br class="hide-mobile" /><br class="hide-mobile" />
</p>
