## Overview

<p>
The Campari Barman Competition is Campari Academy’s annual nationwide contest aimed at selecting the best bartender of the year. <br class="hide-mobile" />
The competition unfolds across three structured phases: an online national call for registrations, in-person selections hosted <br class="hide-mobile" /> at professional bartending schools, and the final live event held at Campari Academy headquarters<br class="show-mobile" /> in Sesto San Giovanni.
</p>

<p>
Our work focused on translating each edition’s creative concept into a coherent campaign system capable of supporting <br class="hide-mobile" /> all stages of the competition, from promotional ADV materials and industry press to digital platform content and final event communication.
</p>

<p>
Deliverables included advertising adaptations for print and outdoor communication, along with concept declinations <br class="hide-mobile" /> and content integration across the official contest digital platform.
</p>
