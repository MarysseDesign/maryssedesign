## Overview

Quantic Milano is a contemporary club based in Milan. <br class="hide-mobile" /> The project required a visual identity able to express energy, modernity and clarity, while remaining flexible across digital formats.

Marysse Studio developed the logo and the digital identity, defining a visual system designed to support social content,<br class="hide-mobile" /> motion graphics <br class="show-mobile" />and online communication with<br class="show-mobile" /> consistency and impact.

## A new club story<br class="show-mobile" /> in Milan

The identity was built around a minimal and modular approach, <br class="hide-mobile" /> starting from the logo and extending to color palette, typography and animated elements.

## Digital identity & visual system

A clean and contemporary visual language designed to support digital communication, <br class="hide-mobile" />
social media content and motion graphics, ensuring flexibility without losing coherence.

<br class="hide-mobile" />

## Motion graphics & digital presence

Motion played a key role in defining<br class="show-mobile" /> the brand’s personality.  
Animated elements were designed to amplify rhythm and energy, translating the club’s atmosphere into a dynamic digital experience.

The system was conceived to be lightweight, repeatable and adaptable across platforms.
