## Overview

Brezza Marina Rooms required a visual identity capable of expressing simplicity,<br class="show-mobile" /> lightness and comfort.  
Marysse Studio developed a visual system designed to reflect the atmosphere of a seaside stay, balancing elegance and informality.

## Visual identity

The logo merges the initial B with sail-inspired forms, seagulls and a wave, <br class="hide-mobile" />transforming the essence of the seaside into a light, fluid and contemporary symbol.

## Packaging & experience design

The identity system was applied to a set of printed materials and packaging elements designed for everyday guest use.<br class="hide-mobile" />
Each item was conceived as part of a cohesive system, reinforcing the brand’s atmosphere <br class="hide-mobile" />through tactile materials, minimal graphics and a clear hierarchy of information.

## A calm and essential visual system

The identity and packaging were designed to feel light and unobtrusive, supporting the guest experience without overwhelming it.<br class="hide-mobile" />
Every element contributes to a sense of ease and continuity, reinforcing the project’s connection to its seaside context.
