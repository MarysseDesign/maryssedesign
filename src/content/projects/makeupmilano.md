## 2018–2020

Development of the brand identity and first digital presence for Make-Up Milano.<br class="hide-mobile" />
The project focused on defining a visual language able to balance elegance and experimentation, <br class="hide-mobile" />
translating the brand’s cosmetic expertise into a contemporary identity system.
