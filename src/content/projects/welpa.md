## The Challenge

<p>
Design a platform capable of addressing a sensitive topic such as home care while ensuring simplicity, transparency and usability.
<br class="hide-mobile" /> The interface needed to feel reassuring and accessible without compromising on efficiency.
</p>

## Digital Identity System and Website Journey

<p>
The digital identity was built by combining warm tones, evoking family, human connection and vitality, with soft lilac and violet hues associated with calmness and reassurance. <br class="hide-mobile" />  This balance was supported by a clear typographic hierarchy and an accessible tone of voice, ensuring clarity and readability across all devices.
</p>

<p>
The website was designed with a clean architecture, intuitive navigation and strong call-to-action hierarchy. <br class="hide-mobile" />
The goal was to reduce friction and make the core actions immediately accessible from the homepage.<br class="hide-mobile" /><br class="hide-mobile" />
</p>
